Inline Equations

Einstein's famous equation $E=mc^2$ relates energy and mass. The Pythagorean theorem states that $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$ for right triangles. The quadratic formula $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$ solves $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$.
